# /scrape-research — Web Research (firecrawl MCP)

Use Firecrawl MCP to research topics relevant to HMN Cascade development.

## Usage
`/scrape-research <topic>` — Research a specific topic
`/scrape-research competitors` — Analyze competing assessment platforms
`/scrape-research <url>` — Scrape and analyze a specific URL

## Available Research Modes

### 1. Competitor Analysis
Research AI readiness assessment platforms:
- Use `firecrawl_search` to find competitors
- Use `firecrawl_scrape` to analyze their features, pricing, UX
- Compare with HMN Cascade capabilities

### 2. Technology Research
Research best practices for:
- AI assessment methodologies
- Conversational AI interview design
- Organizational readiness frameworks
- Scoring and analysis techniques

### 3. URL Scraping
For a specific URL:
- Use `firecrawl_scrape` to extract content
- Summarize key findings
- Identify relevant patterns for the project

### 4. Industry Trends
Search for:
- AI adoption trends in enterprises
- Assessment platform market analysis
- Conversational AI best practices

## Steps

1. Read firecrawl tools: `Read ~/.claude/mcp-tools/servers/firecrawl/TOOLS.md`
2. Use `firecrawl_search` for broad topic discovery
3. Use `firecrawl_scrape` for deep page analysis
4. Use `firecrawl_map` for site structure analysis
5. Synthesize findings into actionable insights for the project

## Output
Provide a structured research brief:
- **Key Findings** — Top 5 insights
- **Competitive Landscape** — How HMN Cascade compares
- **Recommendations** — What to implement/improve
- **Sources** — URLs analyzed
